Our Teachers

We know our Learners and we develop their respect. The relationship between teacher and Learner is a vital element of the learning experience. Therefore, we know what motivates them and their barrier to learning. We know their hobbies and their interests.

Our appropriate use of Summative and Formative assessments (day to day assessments and after end of term assessments), Teaching the Vocabulary, giving Explicit Instructions, usage of Effective Questioning Techniques, Delicate Practice, splitting class in Small groups and Collaborative Learning provides recognition to our work

Our Behaviour Management through classroom discussions (Think, Pair, Share) process gives great feedbacks and understanding among Learners.
